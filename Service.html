Our Services

We are specialized in Overhauling and repair of all types of Reciprocating, Screw, Centrifugal and Rotary Compressors of various makes such as Carrier /Sabroe/Bock/Bitzer/York Johnson control York, Copland, Mitsubishi, Voltas, Grasso, Kirloskar, Alfa-Laval etc. using Original/ Genuine and Imported spares.

We are doing AC Duct Cleaning Services, Marine Air Conditioner and Provision Plant Maintenance Service. Refrigeration units / cold storage cooling system repair.

The jobs are carried out as per Manufacturer specified parameters such as clearances of main and CR bearing Journals, Axial flow of Crank Shaft, Valve plates/ Reeds, Piston rings, Liners/ Pistons and bumping clearance etc.

The Air Conditioning and refrigeration system tune up involves tune up of Thermostatic expansion valves and compressor capacity with loading /unloading to suit the variable Heat Load to ensure smooth and trouble free operation.

We do on system repairs such as Blow through of entire system with high pressure Air, Nitrogen pressure testing, arrest & rectification of leaks, Vaccumizing and recharging of Freon.

We carry out Air distribution balancing & Calibration of HVAC, Cold & Cool Room to ensure optimum performance of system.

We carry out Supply, Fabrication, Installation/ Fitment of Trunking/ Ducting (Circular/ Spiral, Rectangular) which includes straight ducts, bends, offsets, Tees, Reducers, Collars, couplings, dampers, diffusers/ Punka louvers, grills, Air Filters etc
